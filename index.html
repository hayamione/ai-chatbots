Relevance in Today’s Business

In this era where customers are very tech- savvy and always connected to social platforms through their mobile devices. The new generation of customers expects intuitive and seamless customer experience from the companies/business/brands they engage with. They demand near immediate response to their queries. To satisfy these customers and to address the increasing competition in the business, more and more businesses are turning their focus to develop chatbots that can handle customer queries round the clock without any delay.
